Complete Month-Wise Plan

April – November | Strategic Breakdown

2.1 APRIL — Foundation Construction Month

Objective: Eliminate all conceptual gaps. Build daily discipline. Establish baseline score via diagnostic mock.

Week 1 (April 1–7): Diagnostic + System Setup

Day 1 Action: Attempt one full CLAT 2023 paper under timed conditions (2 hours). Score it section-wise. This is your Baseline Score Card.

Weekly Targets:

  • Current Affairs: Begin reading The Hindu daily. Cover March CA summary.
  • English: Read 2 RC passages per day from The Hindu editorial.
  • Legal Reasoning: Study "Legal Principle and Application" methodology.
  • Logical Reasoning: Study Critical Reasoning basics.
  • Quant: Audit your Class 10 math. Identify weak chapters.
Week 2 (April 8–14): Concept Phase Begins
  • English: 3 passages per day. Start attempting questions. Target accuracy: 60% minimum.
  • Current Affairs: Complete February + March monthly CA capsule. Maintain a CA Register.
  • Legal Reasoning: Study 15 Principle-Fact-Application sets per day. Topics: Tort Law principles.
  • Logical Reasoning: 10 questions per day: Assumptions + Inferences.
  • Quant: Ratios, Percentages, Averages — basic level. 15 questions per day.
Week 3 (April 15–21): Acceleration
  • English: 4 passages per day. Begin tracking time per passage and accuracy.
  • Current Affairs: Daily newspaper + maintain CA register. Start Static GK module: Constitution of India.
  • Legal Reasoning: Contract Law principles (offer, acceptance, consideration).
  • Logical Reasoning: Syllogisms + Logical Sequence. 10 questions per day.
  • Quant: Profit/Loss, Simple/Compound Interest. 15 questions per day.
Week 4 (April 22–30): First Mini Assessment
  • Attempt Mock Test 1. Score benchmark: 55–65 out of 120.
  • Legal: Criminal Law principles (IPC basics).
  • Logical: Blood Relations + Seating Arrangement. 20 questions/day.
  • Quant: Time & Work, Speed-Distance-Time. 15 questions/day.

2.2 MAY — Depth Month

Objective: Complete all subject foundations. Increase passage speed. Begin formal mock cycle.

  • Week 1: Legal Deep Dive (Constitutional Law). English: Para-Jumbles. Quant: Data Interpretation.
  • Week 2: Logical Reasoning Deep Dive (Puzzles). Legal: IPR principles.
  • Week 3: Integration Week. Mock Test 2 + 3. English: Speed targeting.
  • Week 4: Consolidation. Mock Test 4. Identify three weakest sub-topics.

May End Benchmark: 70–80 / 120 in mocks.

2.3 JUNE — Speed + Accuracy Month

Objective: Reduce time per question. Increase mock frequency to 2 per week. Begin systematic CA revision.

  • Week 1: English: Push reading speed to 250–280 WPM. Mock Test 5.
  • Weeks 2–4: Mock Tests 6, 7, 8, 9. Legal: Create Principle Summary Cards.

June End Benchmark: 78–88 / 120. Speed: completing paper in 110–115 minutes.

2.4 JULY — Mock Warfare Month

Objective: 3 mocks per week minimum. Deep error analysis. Peak skill development.

Targets: 12–14 total mocks. Maintain Error Log Notebook. January 2024 – July 2025 CA complete coverage.

July End Benchmark: 88–96 / 120.

2.5 AUGUST — Integration + Stamina Month

Objective: Simulate exam conditions daily. Build mental stamina for 2-hour focused performance.

Protocols: Mocks between 3:00 PM – 5:00 PM. 12–16 mocks total. English: 5 passage sets per day.

August End Benchmark: 100–108 / 120 consistently.

2.6 SEPTEMBER — Peak Performance Building

Objective: Achieve consistency above 100. Eliminate variance in scores.

Focus: Variance Reduction. High-Difficulty Exposure (PYQs 2019-2024). Intensive CA revision.

September End Benchmark: 100–110 / 120 with low variance.

2.7 OCTOBER — Consolidation + Intensification

Objective: No new topics. Only reinforcement, speed, and accuracy. Mock frequency 4–5 per week.

Targets: 16–20 mocks. Final Master CA Sheet. Legal: 50 principle sets per day.

October End Benchmark: 108–115 / 120 in 5 consecutive mocks.

2.8 NOVEMBER — War Mode

Objective: Simulate CLAT every single day. Revise everything. Eliminate all remaining weaknesses.

Nov 1–15: Daily Mock + Double Revision Cycle. Revise CA Master Sheet every 3 days.

Nov 16–30: Exam Simulation Fortnight. No new material after Nov 20.

November End Benchmark: Consistent 112–120 / 120 range.
