Daily Timetable — Three Versions

Choose the plan that fits your day

VERSION A: 4-Hour Plan (Minimum Survival Mode)

For days when life interferes — this is the floor, not the standard.

Time Block Duration Activity
6:00 – 6:30 AM 30 min Current Affairs (The Hindu reading + register update)
6:30 – 7:30 AM 60 min English: 2 RC passages (timed) + 10 vocabulary
7:30 – 8:15 AM 45 min Legal Reasoning: 15 principle sets
8:15 – 9:00 AM 45 min Logical Reasoning: 1 puzzle + 10 CR questions
Evening 6:00 – 6:30 PM 30 min Quant: 15 questions
Evening 6:30 – 7:00 PM 30 min CA Register update + Revision of yesterday's notes

Non-Negotiable Even on 4-Hour Days:

  • CA register entry
  • Minimum 2 passages
  • Minimum 10 legal principle sets

VERSION B: 6-Hour Plan (Serious Aspirant — Default Mode)

Time Block Duration Activity
5:30 – 6:00 AM 30 min Wake + Freshen + Review previous night's notes
6:00 – 7:00 AM 60 min Current Affairs (The Hindu full reading + register)
7:00 – 8:30 AM 90 min English: 4 RC passages (timed) + Para-jumbles/Sentence correction
8:45 – 10:15 AM 90 min Legal Reasoning: 25 principle sets (timed: 3 min/set max)
4:00 – 5:00 PM 60 min Logical Reasoning: 2 puzzles + 15 CR questions
5:00 – 5:45 PM 45 min Quantitative Techniques: 20 questions
5:45 – 6:00 PM 15 min Micro-revision: today's CA entries + 1 legal topic summary

Weekly addition: 2 mocks per week (Tuesday + Saturday), replacing 6-hour plan with full 2-hour mock + 2-hour analysis.

VERSION C: 8-Hour Plan (Hardcore Selection Mode)

Time Block Duration Activity
5:00 – 5:30 AM 30 min Wake-up + previous day recall
5:30 – 6:30 AM 60 min Current Affairs: The Hindu + one supplementary source
6:30 – 8:00 AM 90 min English: 5 passages (timed) + grammar drills
8:20 – 10:00 AM 100 min Legal Reasoning: 30–35 principle sets + 15 min PYQ review
10:15 – 11:15 AM 60 min Logical Reasoning: 3 puzzles + 20 CR questions
11:15 AM – 12:00 PM 45 min Quantitative Techniques: 25 questions
1:00 – 2:00 PM 60 min CA: Static GK / Constitutional revision / Monthly CA deep study
2:00 – 3:00 PM 60 min Weak subject dedicated drilling
3:00 – 5:00 PM 120 min Mock days: Full Mock (2:00–4:00 PM)
5:00 – 7:00 PM 120 min Mock Analysis (mock days) OR Subject Revision (non-mock days)
7:00 – 7:30 PM 30 min CA Register final update + day's error log entry

Rest Protocol:

  • Hard stop at 9:00 PM.
  • Sleep by 10:00 PM maximum.
  • Sleep deprivation is the single biggest performance killer.
