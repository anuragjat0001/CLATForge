Revision System

Distributed revision for maximum retention

Weekly Revision (Every Sunday — 90 Minutes)

Time Activity
0:00 – 0:30CA register: full week's entries. Self-quiz format.
0:30 – 0:55Legal: Skim week's principle sets. Re-do any 5 you got wrong.
0:55 – 1:15English: Re-read any 2 passages from the week. Check annotated vocabulary.
1:15 – 1:30Error log review: categorize week's errors from mock/practice.

Monthly Revision (Last 2 Days — 4 Hours Total)

Day 1 (3 Hours):

  • Hour 1: Complete CA register for the month. Update One-Liner Master Sheet.
  • Hour 2: All Legal Reasoning principle sets from the month — rapid review.
  • Hour 3: English — review vocabulary log for the month.

Day 2 (1 Hour):

  • Mock Test under timed conditions (monthly assessment).
  • Compare with previous month's mock score. Set next month's target.

Last 30 Days Strategy (MOST CRITICAL)

Rule 1: No New Material

Starting November 15, you must NOT attempt any new topic, new source, or new concept.

Rule 2: Mock-Analyze-Revise Cycle

Daily: Full Mock (3:00–5:00 PM), 2 hours analysis, 1.5 hours targeted revision, 1 hour CA Master Sheet.

Rule 3: Rotating CA Revision

Divide CA Master Sheet into 8 sections. Cover one full section per day in rotation.

Rule 4: The Final 7 Days

No full mocks. Only section-wise mini-tests. 3 hours CA revision per day. 1 hour Legal speed drills. Physical rest priority.
