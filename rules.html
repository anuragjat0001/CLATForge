Hardcore Rules (Non-Negotiable)

The Zero Compromise Framework

Daily Minimum Tasks — The Absolute Floor

These 4 tasks must be completed EVERY day without exception:

  • CA Register Entry: Minimum 30 minutes of The Hindu reading and notes.
  • English Passages: Minimum 2 RC passages per day, timed.
  • Legal Reasoning: Minimum 10 principle-application sets.
  • Error Log Update: If a mock was taken, minimum 30-minute analysis.

If any of these 4 tasks are not completed, that day is classified as a ZERO DAY.

Zero Day Protocol

  • Do not attempt to compensate by doubling the next day's load impulsively.
  • The next day: Complete that day's normal tasks PLUS the 4 minimum tasks of the missed day.
  • Record the Zero Day in your progress tracker.
  • More than 2 Zero Days in one week triggers a mandatory week review.

Non-Negotiable Daily Rules

1. Phone is not accessible

During any study block. Use app blockers if necessary (Forest, FocusMode, Cold Turkey).

2. Fixed physical location

Study in one fixed physical location only. Do not study on your bed.

3. Specific objectives

Begin each study session by writing 3 specific objectives. End by writing 3 things accomplished.

4. No peer comparison

Do not discuss CLAT preparation with peers daily. Peer comparison is a morale destabilizer.

5. No source changes

Do not change your source materials mid-preparation. Changing series destroys consistency.

The Discipline System

Weekly Accountability Check (Self-administered, Every Sunday):

  1. How many of the 7 days this week had all 4 minimum tasks completed?
  2. What was my mock count this week vs. target?
  3. Did I complete my revision schedule?
  4. What was my average mock score this week?
